How to Manage Reports

A quick guide to handling user-reported issues.

View Details

Click the View button to examine report details, attached images, and existing admin notes.

Update Status

Once resolved, add a management note and click Mark as done. The system will automatically log your ID.

Filter & Search

Search reports by Title or ID, or use the Status filter to focus on pending tasks (Not Done).
